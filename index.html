The Cellular Jail, alsos known as Kala Pani, is a colonial prison in Port Blair. It is a national memorial and an important historical site, symbolizing the struggle for India's independence. The jail is now a museum showcasing the history and stories of the freedom fighters imprisoned there.

Ross Island is a small island near Port Blair. It was once the administrative headquarters of the British colony in the Andaman Islands. The island is known for its ruins of colonial buildings, lush greenery, and wildlife, making it a popular tourist destination.

The Makruzz is a high-speed catamaran ferry that operates between the islands of Andaman, including Port Blair, Havelock Island, and Neil Island. It is known for its comfort and speed, providing a popular mode of transportation for tourists traveling between these islands.
